Introduction

Steam & Stone

Steam & Stone is a digital magazine dedicated to the world of coffee and its culture. It highlights independent roasters, barista artistry, various brewing methods, and the cultures that thrive around cafés in different communities. This publication isn't about mainstream chains, but instead celebrates the passionate local roasters that shape a new wave of coffee appreciation.
